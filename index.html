About InterviewAI

We're on a mission to democratize interview preparation and help everyone land their dream job.

Our Mission

InterviewAI was founded with a simple belief: everyone deserves the opportunity to present their best self in an interview. Traditional interview preparation is expensive, time-consuming, and often inaccessible to those who need it most.

By leveraging advanced AI and speech recognition technology, we've created a platform that provides personalized, professional interview coaching at a fraction of the cost—available 24/7, whenever you need it.

How We Help

Our AI-powered platform analyzes thousands of successful interview responses to provide you with:

  • Real-time feedback on your answers
  • Industry-specific interview questions
  • Personalized improvement recommendations
  • Voice and text practice modes
  • Detailed performance analytics
